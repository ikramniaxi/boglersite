Boggler assistTM Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to redbutton.ai’s boggler Assist™, the app that uses generative AI to help you summarize your interactions effectively. At redbutton.ai, we place a high value on your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application, boggler assist™, and visit our website.

Information We Collect

We collect and process the following categories of data about you:

  • Information you provide: When you sign up for boggler assist™, we may collect your name and email address.
  • To improve our services: By analyzing how boggler assist™ is used, we continually enhance your experience.
  • To communicate with you: We may contact you about your account, updates, or other service-related matters.

Information Sharing

We prioritize your privacy and do not sell or share your personal data for third-party marketing purposes without your consent. However, we may share your information in the following situations:

  • Service providers: We may share data with third parties who provide services on our behalf, including data analysis, hosting, customer support, and marketing assistance.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, such as in response to a court order or subpoena.

Security

Your data's security is paramount. We implement robust technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized processing, loss, destruction, or damage.

Children's Privacy

boggler assist™ is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

Data Retention

Your privacy is important to us. Please be assured that the recordings, transcripts, and summaries of your interactions in boggler assist™ remain entirely private and are only accessible by you. We do not access or share this content with any third parties.

We retain certain performance statistics and rating responses solely for the purpose of improving our services and enhancing your experience. These statistics and responses do not contain any personally identifiable information and are used to help us understand how boggler assist™ is performing and how we can make it even better.

Rest assured, you are the only person who has a secure connection to your data, ensuring the utmost privacy and confidentiality of your interactions.

Your data remains under your control, and we are committed to maintaining the security and privacy of your information.

User Control

You have the ability to access, update, or delete your data through our user-friendly controls.

Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies and tracking technologies. You can manage or opt-out of tracking through your device settings.

Data Transfer

If your data is transferred internationally, rest assured that we maintain data protection standards across borders.

User Consent

We obtain your consent, especially when processing sensitive data like audio recordings.

User Rights

You have rights concerning your data, such as access, rectification, or deletion, as applicable under data protection regulations.

Account Downgrade or Termination

If you choose to downgrade or terminate your boggler assist™ account, please note the following data retention policy:

  • We will provide you with access to your "assists" and "interactions" for a period of 30 days after your account is downgraded or terminated. During this time, you can retrieve your data.
  • After the 30-day period has elapsed, your "assists" and "interactions" will be permanently deleted from our system. This means that should you decide to re-establish your boggler assist™ service 31 days or later after downgrading or terminating your account, your previous data will not be available.

    • We encourage you to ensure you have saved any data you wish to retain within this 30-day window if you plan to discontinue your account temporarily or permanently. Your data privacy is important to us, and we want to make this transition as clear and seamless as possible for you.

Data Breach Notification

We have a procedure for notifying you and relevant authorities in case of a data breach.

Compliance

We comply with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable.

Third-Party Services

Third-party services used in boggler assist™ are subject to their respective privacy policies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy, and any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data handling practices, please contact us at boggler@redbutton.ai.
